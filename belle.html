Belle

Directed by Mamoru Hosoda
Released: July, 2021

Posted by Fausto Vidrio on November 30, 2022

Summary:

A shy high school student named Suzu lives in rural village in Japan. Although she lives with her dad and is close friends with a few of her schoolmates, she has very low self-esteem. But when she enters "U", a massive virtual world run by avatars, she becomes a famed singer known as Belle and encounters an avatar in need of her help known as "The Beast".

Relative thoughts of the movie:

The things about the film that really stand out to me are the character development, the plot of the story, and the movie soundtrack.


I enjoyed learning more about the characters' personalities and background stories as the film progressed. Giving some time to show events that happened in the character's past is what the film had done really well because it gives the audience more insight into why a character behaves a certain way in the film.


I believe that the character development also helped into building the plot of the story. The film contains a subplot within the main plot that has a high resemblance to the disney film Beauty and the Beast.Considering the names of the two main characters "Belle" and "The Beast", it is really hard not to notice the correlation. Nonetheless, I think the story was beautiful.


The film provides a great musical soundtrack that goes really well with the singing of the main character Belle. I think the greatest thing for the directors to do for the film was to produce japanese and english versions of each song.
