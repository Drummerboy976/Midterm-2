Spirited Away

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki
Released: July, 2001

Posted by Fausto Vidrio on November 30, 2022

Summary:

A young girl named Chihiro and her family are moving to a new home but have lost their way to an abandoned theme park. After seeing her parents turned into beasts from eating sacred food, chihiro has found herself in a mysterious world ruled by gods, witches, and spirits. It is now her duty to find a way to turn her parents back to humans and leave the world of the spirits.

Relative thoughts of the movie:

There are two things that I really like about this movie. The first one being the plot development. The second one being the animation quality.


In regard to the plot, I think the story provided a lot of dramatic and beautiful events that happen to or around the main character. The film includes a wide variety of support characters to help build the story.


Considering that the film was released in 2001, I think the film's animation quality was ahead of its time. The film animators show this by excellent details and visual effects seen in the film.
